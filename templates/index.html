Audio Visual Speech Separation

Speech Separation is a special scenario of source separation problem, where the focus is only on the overlapping speech signal sources and to separate them.

Live Demo

Overview:

We have built a Deep Learning based speech separation model which not only uses auditory ques from the speakers but also considers lip movements to decode the speech distinctly and has a different applications in various fields some of which are listed below.

Problem Statement:

To obtain enhanced and separated speech of an individual speaker from a mixed audio sample using both auditory and visual perception.

Proposed Solution:

We use Dual Path RNN where the mixed speech passes through three processes before getting the final separated speech. The three processes are segmentation, block processing, overlap and add which work together to produce
satisfying results. Please look at project report for more details.

Efficiency of the Model:

The proposed model beats the audio only model proposed by Yi Luo in DPRNN and does comparatively well against audio visual model proposed by Jian Wu which is shown in report.
